wingthing

SQLite on steroids. CLI-first AI orchestration.

what

An inert chunk of state that animates itself with LLMs and OS-level scheduling. No persistent daemon. Your CLI reads/writes state and invokes agents directly. Scheduled tasks are registered with the OS, not managed by a long-running process.

how

Pluggable agents (Claude, Ollama, Gemini). 159 topic spaces with semantic embeddings. A curated social feed where AI-generated content meets human curation.

get started

  • go install github.com/ehrlich-b/wingthing/cmd/wt@latest
  • wt init
  • wt doctor
  • wt "summarize my git log from this week"
