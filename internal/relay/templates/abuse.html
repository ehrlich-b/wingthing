abuse policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

reporting abuse

If you believe a wingthing user or wing is being used for abuse, report it to abuse@wingthing.ai. Include as much detail as possible.

what counts as abuse

  • Distributing malware, ransomware, or other malicious software
  • Unauthorized access to systems or data
  • Using the relay to proxy attacks or exfiltrate data
  • Circumventing security measures or rate limits
  • Harassment or harm directed at other users

enforcement

We may suspend or terminate accounts and wing connections at our discretion. Repeat or severe violations result in permanent bans. See the terms of service for the full acceptable use policy.

security vulnerabilities

If you find a security issue in wingthing, report it to security@wingthing.ai. We take these seriously and will respond promptly.
